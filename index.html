What is Botany?

Most simply stated, Botany is the study of plants. Botany is a branch of biology which is specialized in plant physiology, structure, genetics, ecology, classification, and more. Botany is important for so many reasons, one important reason is growing crops. Botany can ensure that the yeild is large enough. Moreover botany can help with medicines, specific plants hold medicinal value which can later help individuals.

What are the Parts of a Plant?

Like most living creatures, plants need a way to reproduce. Some plants only have an ovule, and other plants just have a stamen, and in some cases plants have both reproductive parts. The stamen has the pollen which when spread to an ovule can help a plant reproduce. In order to attract pollinators plants produce nectar in the nectaries, which can be located in an any part of the plant. Plants also have roots, which have the responsibility of giving the plant nutrients and water. Plants often have pentals which also contribute to attracting pollinators.

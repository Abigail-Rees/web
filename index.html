What is Botany?

Most simply stated, Botany is the study of plants. Botany is a branch of biology which specialized in plant physiology, structure, genetics, ecology, classification, and more. Botany is important for so many reasons, one important reason is growing crops. Botany can ensure that the yeild is large enough. Moreover botany can help with medicines, specific plants hold medicinal value which can later help individuals.
